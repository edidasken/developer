A Complete Suite of Ministry Tools, Built for the Work of the Church

Everything a pastor touches in the course of his week — in one integrated system, on any device, completely free.

By the FlockOS Team  ·  flock-os.github.io

FlockOS is not a product. It is a calling made software. Built for the local church pastor — not for a denomination, not for a megachurch with an IT department, but for the man in the room with the people, carrying the weight of their names, their struggles, their prayers, and their growth in grace.

It brings together everything a pastor touches in the course of his week — member directory, pastoral notes, prayer requests, small group rosters, worship planning, outreach tools, discipleship resources, and church communication — into one integrated system that runs on any device, in any browser, from anywhere in the world.

"FlockOS carries the weight of the administration so the pastor can carry the weight of the people — and it is completely free, because the Gospel is."

A Foundation Built on Grace, Not Commerce

Church management software has historically been priced as if the church is a business and the pastor is a product manager. FlockOS was built on a different conviction. There is no subscription fee. No per-seat pricing. No features locked behind an upgrade wall. Every module. Every update. Every capability — free. The resources of a large church deserve to be in the hands of the smallest congregation.

The pastor of twelve should have the same tools as the pastor of twelve hundred. "Freely you have received; freely give." — Matthew 10:8

No Server. No IT. No Barriers.

FlockOS is a web application that runs in any modern browser on any device. There is nothing to install, no server to maintain, no IT team required. It is hosted for free on GitHub Pages — an infrastructure maintained by Microsoft — serving churches 24 hours a day with no monthly fees and no expiration date.

For pastors without reliable internet access, The Wellspring mode allows the complete church database to run offline on a single encrypted file — no connectivity required. FlockOS was designed to go wherever the Great Commission sends you.

Your Data. Your Accounts. Your Church.

The design keeps your church's data in your own Google or Firebase accounts — not in a central FlockOS-owned database. You own it. You control it. If you stop using FlockOS tomorrow, your data is still yours. There is no exit fee. No hostage situation.

Sensitive pastoral records — counseling notes, care cases, prayer journals — are protected by role-based access control. Six permission levels, from Guest to Admin, ensure that your private conversations with a struggling family never appear on the welcome desk screen.

The FlockOS Suite  ·  Eight Apps, One Mission

FlockOS — The Shepherd's Dashboard
Member directory, pastoral care notes, prayer tracking, small groups, events, attendance, spiritual milestones, and service planning — all in one place.
GROW — Discipleship Hub
Gospel presentation, one-year Bible plan, devotionals, Strong's lexicon, theology, apologetics, and missions data for 238 nations. Public. No account required.
The Invitation — Gospel Page
One page. One message. A clear presentation of Jesus — who He is, what He did, how to respond. Share it with a seeker in a text or a counseling conversation.
Flock Stand — The Music Stand
Song library, setlist builder, chord transposer, and live lyrics display. The entire worship team, on the same page, in any key, with or without internet.
FlockChat — Church Messaging
Secure real-time messaging — team channels, small group rooms, direct messages, and prayer chains. Your congregation's conversations stay in your church's accounts.
FlockShow — Worship Display
Slides for your service — lyrics, Scripture, announcements — pulling directly from your setlists and service plans. No manual recreation every week.
FEED — Sermon Workspace
Outline your message, track your series, and build a searchable archive of every sermon ever preached — connected directly to your service plans.
The Wellspring — Offline Mode
The full suite, running from a single encrypted file with zero internet required. Built for the missionary, the church planter, and the pastor at the edge of the world.
