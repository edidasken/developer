FlockOS — A Pastor's Guide

"I am the vine; you are the branches." — John 15:5

What FlockOS Is

FlockOS is a complete church management and ministry platform — built specifically for pastors who understand that ministry is not a workflow problem, but a calling. It brings together every dimension of church life into one integrated system: member care, pastoral notes, prayer requests, small groups, events, attendance, giving, discipleship resources, missions data, worship planning, and real-time communication.

Think of it as the operating system for your church — not just software that manages a contact list, but a full infrastructure that grows with you from your first twenty members to your five hundredth, carrying the story of every soul God brings through your doors.

Why It Was Built

Church management software has always been priced as if the church is a business and the pastor is a product manager. FlockOS was built by someone who understands that pastoral ministry is different. The goal wasn't to automate the pastor out of the picture — it was to hand him back hours, so that when he sits with the grieving widow on a Wednesday afternoon, he isn't mentally carrying the weight of fifty things he hasn't tracked yet.

FlockOS carries the weight so the pastor can carry the people.

The Foundation: Free, Because the Gospel Is Free

No subscription fees. No per-seat pricing. No "upgrade to unlock pastoral care." Every feature. Every module. Every update. Free.

The resources of a large church deserve to be in the hands of the smallest congregation. The pastor of twelve should have the same tools as the pastor of twelve hundred. "Freely you have received; freely give." (Matthew 10:8)

How FlockOS Works — No IT Required

FlockOS is a web-based progressive web app that runs in any modern browser on any device — desktop, tablet, or phone. There's no software to install, no server to maintain, and no IT team required.

Three Backend Options (Choose What Fits Your Church)

FlockOS meets churches where they are — technically and geographically. You choose the backend that fits your situation:

1. Google Sheets + Apps Script (The Original Engine)

Your church data lives in a Google Spreadsheet that you own and control. Google Apps Script serves it as a free API. No servers. No hosting fees. Just a spreadsheet. This works anywhere smartphones can reach Google Drive.

2. Firebase Firestore (Real-Time Cloud)

Real-time, cloud-native. Member data, messaging, prayer chains, and pastoral care all sync live across devices. Scales from a church of 12 to a network of 12,000. Most churches stay within Firebase's free tier and pay $0.

3. The Wellspring (Fully Offline)

No internet. No cloud. Your entire church in one encrypted file, loaded directly into the browser. For the pastor the internet hasn't reached yet. Export your database once, load it into FlockOS, and the full app runs entirely offline with zero connectivity required.

Free Hosting via GitHub Pages

FlockOS is hosted for free on GitHub Pages — a service provided by GitHub at no cost. Your church gets a permanent web address that serves your FlockOS deployment 24/7, with no monthly fees, no bandwidth charges, and no expiration.

Security You Can Trust

  • 256-bit AES-GCM encryption with PBKDF2 key derivation for offline data vaults
  • Role-based access control with 6 permission levels (Guest, Member, Leader, Deacon, Pastor, Admin)
  • PIN-protected offline vaults — the encryption key is never stored; only the right PIN unlocks member data
  • Firebase Security Rules enforce strict data access — members can only see what their role permits
  • No third-party tracking — your church data stays with you, not with advertisers

The FlockOS App Family

FlockOS is not one app — it's a complete suite of ministry tools, each designed to serve a specific calling in the life of the church. Every app can be installed as a standalone progressive web app on any device.

1. FlockOS — The Shepherd's Dashboard

What It Does: FlockOS is the heart of the system — your complete church management platform. Member directory with photos and family connections, pastoral care notes, prayer request tracking, small groups, events, attendance, giving records, spiritual milestones (salvations, baptisms, dedications), service planning, announcements, analytics, and more.

"Shepherd the flock of God that is among you, exercising oversight, not under compulsion, but willingly, as God would have you; not for shameful gain, but eagerly; not domineering over those in your charge, but being examples to the flock."

— 1 Peter 5:2-3 (ESV)

FlockOS exists to help you shepherd well — to remember the names, the stories, the prayers, and the moments that make up the life of your flock.

2. GROW — Public Discipleship & Outreach

What It Does: GROW is your church's public-facing ministry page — a beautiful, Gospel-centered resource hub you can share with anyone. Daily devotionals, one-year Bible plans, Strong's Lexicon, apologetics, systematic theology, world missions data, Bible quizzes, pastoral counseling resources, and teaching plans. No account required.

"Go therefore and make disciples of all nations, baptizing them in the name of the Father and of the Son and of the Holy Spirit, teaching them to observe all that I have commanded you."

— Matthew 28:19-20 (ESV)

GROW exists to make disciples — not just to inform, but to transform.

3. Flock Stand — The Worship Team's Music Stand

What It Does: Complete digital music stand for your worship team. Song library, setlist builder, transposition, Nashville Number System, live presenter mode. Everything your worship leader needs in one app.

"Speaking to one another with psalms, hymns, and songs from the Spirit. Sing and make music from your heart to the Lord, always giving thanks to God the Father for everything, in the name of our Lord Jesus Christ."

— Ephesians 5:19-20 (NIV)

Flock Stand exists to help your worship team lead well — to prepare with excellence and create space for the Spirit to move.

4. The Invitation — A Gospel Presentation

What It Does: A standalone Gospel presentation page — a clear, compelling walk through who Jesus is, what He offers, and how to respond. Designed to be shared with seekers, new believers, or anyone who needs to hear the good news.

"For I am not ashamed of the gospel, because it is the power of God that brings salvation to everyone who believes: first to the Jew, then to the Gentile."

— Romans 1:16 (NIV)

The Invitation exists to proclaim Christ — clearly, boldly, without apology.

5. FlockChat — Real-Time Church Messaging

What It Does: Your church's private messaging platform — secure, real-time communication for your team, small groups, and ministry leaders. Public channels, private rooms, direct messages, prayer chains with status tracking, role-based access, and push notifications.

"And let us consider how we may spur one another on toward love and good deeds, not giving up meeting together, as some are in the habit of doing, but encouraging one another—and all the more as you see the Day approaching."

— Hebrews 10:24-25 (NIV)

FlockChat exists to build fellowship — to connect the scattered, to encourage the weary, and to keep the body moving together.

6. FlockShow — Church Presentation & Worship Display

What It Does: Your church's presentation tool — build and display slides for worship lyrics, sermon points, announcements, and Scripture. Designed specifically for church services with clean layouts and distraction-free display.

"Until I come, devote yourself to the public reading of Scripture, to preaching and to teaching."

— 1 Timothy 4:13 (NIV)

FlockShow exists to help you proclaim the Word — to display it clearly and keep the focus where it belongs.

7. Bread Maker (FEED) — Sermon Preparation

What It Does: Your sermon preparation workspace — a place to craft the Word with clarity and depth. Outline your message, store notes, track sermon series, and prepare to feed your flock well.

"Preach the word; be prepared in season and out of season; correct, rebuke and encourage—with great patience and careful instruction."

— 2 Timothy 4:2 (NIV)

Bread Maker exists to help you prepare well — to dig deep, to think clearly, and to deliver the Word with faithfulness.

8. The Wellspring — Offline Data Management

What It Does: FlockOS's local-first data engine — the tool that makes full offline ministry possible. Export your entire church database into one encrypted file, load it into FlockOS, and the full app runs entirely offline. No internet required.

"Whoever drinks the water I give them will never thirst. Indeed, the water I give them will become in them a spring of water welling up to eternal life."

— John 4:14 (NIV)

The Wellspring exists to ensure that no pastor is left without tools — no matter where he is, no matter how remote, no matter how disconnected.

The Bottom Line for Pastors

FlockOS is not a product. It's a calling made software. It was built to serve you — to give you back time, to help you remember the people God has entrusted to you, and to support the ministry He's placed on your shoulders.

It's free because the Gospel is free. It's offline-capable because the Great Commission doesn't stop at the edge of the internet. It's integrated because the body of Christ doesn't work in silos.

Use it well. Shepherd your flock. Make disciples. Proclaim Christ.
